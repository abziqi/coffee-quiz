# Coffee Personality Quiz — Requirements

## Personality → Coffee Pairings

| Personality | Coffee | Tagline |
|-------------|--------|---------|
| Bold Adventurer | Double Espresso | "You live for intensity" |
| Sweet Enthusiast | Caramel Latte | "Life's too short for bitter" |
| Zen Minimalist | Black Coffee, Single Origin | "Simple. Clean. Perfect." |
| Indulgent Treat | Mocha with Whip | "Coffee is dessert" |

## Result Display
- **Single recommendation only** — user gets their top personality + coffee match
- Example: "You're a Zen Minimalist! Your coffee: Black Coffee, Single Origin."

## Visual Style
- **Warm & cozy** (Style 4)
- Earth tones, soft gradients, inviting feel
- Font: Lora (headings) + Nunito (body)
- Colors: warm creams, browns, soft oranges (#FFFBF6, #C8813A, #EDD5B3, #3D2B1F)
- Rounded corners, soft shadows

## Graphics
- SVG illustrations for each coffee result (one per personality type)
- No icons on answer options — text only for clean look

## Quiz Questions

**Q1: How do you take your coffee?**
- Black, no nonsense → Zen Minimalist
- With a splash of milk → Bold Adventurer
- Sweet and creamy → Sweet Enthusiast
- Extra everything, make it a treat → Indulgent Treat

**Q2: Hot or iced?**
- Always hot — it's about the ritual → Zen Minimalist
- Iced, even in winter → Bold Adventurer
- Hot with a flavored syrup → Sweet Enthusiast
- Blended or iced with whip → Indulgent Treat

**Q3: How many coffees do you have a day?**
- One, carefully chosen → Zen Minimalist
- Two or three, minimum → Bold Adventurer
- Just one, but it has to be delicious → Sweet Enthusiast
- However many I need to feel like a dessert → Indulgent Treat

**Q4: What's your go-to order at a new café?**
- Whatever single origin they're proud of → Zen Minimalist
- Double shot, straight → Bold Adventurer
- Something with caramel or vanilla → Sweet Enthusiast
- The most indulgent thing on the menu → Indulgent Treat

**Q5: What's your relationship with espresso?**
- It's art. Respect it. → Zen Minimalist
- Fuel. Pure fuel. → Bold Adventurer
- Love it in a latte → Sweet Enthusiast
- Only if it's in a mocha → Indulgent Treat

**Q6: Pick a coffee moment:**
- Early morning, silent house, perfect cup → Zen Minimalist
- Pre-workout, ready to attack the day → Bold Adventurer
- Afternoon café date with a friend → Sweet Enthusiast
- Cozy evening treat, dessert vibes → Indulgent Treat
